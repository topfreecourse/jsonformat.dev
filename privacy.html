Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

JSONFormat.dev provides a set of browser-based tools to format, validate, minify, repair, and beautify JSON data. Your privacy matters to us, and we aim to ensure that your data remains safe and protected while using our service.

2. No Data Collection

We do not store, log, or transmit any of your input data. Every JSON-related action is executed directly within your browser, with no data sent to any server. Your content stays on your device and is never sent to any server controlled by us.

3. No Tracking or Cookies

JSONFormat.dev does not use tracking cookies, advertisements, or third-party analytics that monitor your behavior. We avoid invasive scripts and do not profile or fingerprint our users.

4. Use of Third-Party Libraries

Some features of our site utilize open-source libraries (e.g., CodeMirror for syntax highlighting). These libraries run locally and do not send your data to any third-party service.

5. Optional Analytics

To understand usage patterns and improve our service, we may use privacy-friendly analytics tools that do not collect personal data or user-submitted JSON. These tools collect only aggregated usage metrics (like total page views).

6. File Uploads

If you use the file upload feature, your file contents are handled only within your browser for processing and are never transmitted to any external server or saved beyond your active session.

7. Security Measures

We follow best practices by ensuring all processing happens client-side. No JSON, files, or input data leave your browser unless you explicitly choose to copy or share the result yourself.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy occasionally to align with new features or legal obligations. You’re encouraged to check this page from time to time. By continuing to use the site, you agree to the latest version of the policy.
